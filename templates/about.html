About the College :

Guntur Engineering College popularly known as GEC was established in the year 2008 in Yanamadala Village, Prathipadu Mandal of Guntur district, Andhra Pradesh.GEC is located away from the hustle and bustle of the city life in a scenic and serene environment spread over an expansive 10.77 acres amidst the lush greenery cotton fields. GEC is a unique professional college in the state of Andhra Pradesh. GEC is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada (JNTUK) and approved by AICTE.With a steadfast vision to offer quality education to its students, GEC has evolved into a beacon of excellence in the field of education. At GEC, education is not just a process, but a journey towards fostering intellectual growth and innovation. The institution takes pride in offering a diverse range of academic programs, including 4 B.Tech Programs in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering (AI & ML), Computer Science & Engineering ( Data Science), and 2 PG Programs, M.C.A. and M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering.
Over 15 years, the college has achieved significant success and recognition. One of the notable strengths of the college is its visionary management. The management team is known for its strategic planning, adequate documentation, record-keeping practices, and implementation of e-management systems. The Institution also adopts innovative teaching practices aligned with global trends, emphasizing quality and access in the curriculum and teaching methodologies. GEC have well-ventilated classrooms, multimodal teaching methodologies, campus placement training, a dedicated canteen, a fleet of buses and a mineral water plant and power backup with generators.
GEC goes beyond conventional education by offering various add-on and certificate courses that help students in upgrading their skills and staying relevant in a rapidly changing world. The institution has fostered meaningful collaborations with numerous industries through Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) with Skill oriented Companies such as CodegnanDestination , allowing students to gain real-time knowledge and exposure to industry practices. GEC imparts Outcome Based Education (OBE) which gives equal opportunities to teaching and learning curricular, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.
The campus is equipped with fire safety equipment, Monitored by security cameras, and is manned by numerous security personnel. To help students with their supplementary education various Cells Such as Training and Placement, Entrepreneurship development cell, etc., introduced and these cells helps the students for access to expert career counselling and are aided in their search for internships and placements. To ensure the holistic development of its students, GEC promotes sports and extracurricular activities through various committees. The institution also places great emphasis on inculcating human values, encouraging both staff members and students to participate in social activities organized by the NSS unit.
